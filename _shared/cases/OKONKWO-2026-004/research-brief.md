## Market Conditions — Hyde Park / Rosedale / Brentwood (Feb 2026)

**Days on market:** 8–16 days for well-priced homes

## Price Range Assessment
For a 3BR character home with a yard in these three neighborhoods:
- **Realistic range:** $610,000–$660,000

## Neighborhood Character Notes
**Hyde Park:** Most walkable of the three. Avenue B grocery, restaurants on Duval and 45th. Quieter streets, mature oak canopy. Mix of longtime owners and younger families. Where the Okonkwos should focus first.
**Rosedale:** Slightly larger lots, more variation in home condition. Further from walkable amenities. Higher upside on lot size.
**Brentwood:** More affordable end of the three, slightly more suburban feel. Good value play if Hyde Park/Rosedale come up empty.
