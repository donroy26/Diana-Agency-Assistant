## Walkability Assessment

**Mueller (78723):** Walk Score 82 — Very Walkable. HEB grocery 0.4mi. Alamo Drafthouse, Uchi, numerous coffee shops and restaurants within the Mueller development itself. Designed as a walkable neighborhood. Light rail access. This is Priya's best option given the no-car constraint.

**East Austin 78702:** Walk Score 76 — Very Walkable. Strongest walkability in the Chestnut/Holly pocket near E 6th and E 7th corridors. Less consistent than Mueller. Walkability depends heavily on which block.

**Recommendation: Prioritize Mueller.** The infrastructure is purpose-built for car-free living in a way East Austin is not.
