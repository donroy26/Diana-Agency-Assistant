## Market Reality Check — Budget vs. Location
This is the most important finding in this brief: **$375k is at the floor of the Bouldin Creek/SoCo market for a 1BR condo with character.**

Current active 1BR condos in Bouldin Creek and SoCo corridor:
- Average list price: $368,000
- Range: $342k (dated, needs work) to $412k (updated, move-in)
- At $375k, Derek is competing for dated units or small square footage (under 700 sqft)

**This budget does not buy what he described.** A 1BR with character, a patio, and walkable location in Bouldin Creek is a $385–420k asset in the current market.

## What $375k Gets in This Market
- A 1BR condo, 620–720 sqft, likely dated (original finishes, older appliances)
- OR a 1BR in a good location that needs $15–25k in updates
- Outdoor space at this price point is rare. Patio units command a premium.
- Character (vintage buildings, bungalow conversions) runs $380k+

---

## Alternative to Consider
If Derek can expand his search radius 10 minutes south to 78745 (St. Elmo / Oltorf area), his budget gets him significantly more: 2BR bungalows in the $355–380k range, walkable to the St. Elmo district (breweries, restaurants), a different vibe but genuine character.

---

## Recommendation for Agent
Present the market reality directly but not discourageingly. Derek may not know his budget is tight for this specific submarket. Options: (1) expand search to adjacent neighborhoods, (2) adjust budget ceiling, (3) accept smaller/dated unit and plan updates. Do not push him toward a decision. He said he's not on a timeline.
