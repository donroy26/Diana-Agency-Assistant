## Neighborhood Notes
**78702 (Central East Austin — Chestnut, Holly, Govalle):** Most walkable of the East Austin zip codes. Closest to downtown amenities. Coffee, restaurants, bars within walking distance. Higher price per sqft. This is where the Chens' wishlist lands them.

**78721 (East Austin — MLK, Rosewood):** Slightly more affordable, slightly less walkable. More families, fewer 20-somethings. Good for the longer-term given the baby coming.

**Recommendation:** Start in 78702, expand to 78721 if nothing fits budget.

---

## Home Office Note
In this price range, a 3/2 will typically have one primary bedroom, one secondary, and a third that functions as an office or nursery (8x10 to 10x12). Confirm with clients whether "home office" means dedicated separate room or a bedroom that doubles as office.

---

## Red Flags to Watch
- Foundation issues are common in East Austin clay soil. Always inspect.
- Older homes (pre-1980) may have original electrical. Factor in upgrade cost.
- Some blocks in 78702 have commercial noise from East 6th corridor. Do a Saturday night drive-by.
