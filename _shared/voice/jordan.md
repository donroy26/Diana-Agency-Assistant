# Voice Profile: Jordan
**Status: ACTIVE**
**Built:** 2026-05-10

## Tone
Enthusiastic, detail-oriented, eager to prove competence. Six months in. Still finding her footing on tone but sharp on process. Clients appreciate her responsiveness and thoroughness. Diana is coaching her to be less apologetic in her language.

## Sentence Style
Slightly longer than necessary. She over-explains when nervous. Getting better. Active voice. Tends to front-load context before the main point. Responds fast.

## How They Open Messages
- "Hi [Name]," — standard
- "Thanks so much for your patience" — appears when she's been slower than usual
- "Just following up on —" for check-ins
- "Great news!" when delivering positive updates

## How They Close Messages
- "Please let me know if you have any questions!" — default (Diana has flagged to use less)
- "Jordan" — always signs full name
- Ends with next steps spelled out in detail

## Signature Phrases
- "I wanted to make sure..."
- "Just to clarify..."
- "Happy to answer any questions!"
- "As a next step..."

## What to Avoid
- Apologetic openers ("Sorry for the delay," "I apologize if this is confusing")
- Hedging every statement ("I think," "I believe," "maybe")
- Over-promising on timelines
- Exclamation points on every sentence (limit to one per message)

## Sample Line
Hi Priya, I wanted to make sure you had the research brief on Mueller vs. East Austin. I've put together a comparison based on what you told me matters most. As a next step, let me know which neighborhoods you'd like to schedule tours in and I'll get those set up.
