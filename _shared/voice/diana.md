# Voice Profile: Diana
**Status: ACTIVE**
**Built:** 2026-05-01

## Tone
Confident, warm, no-nonsense. Eight years of Austin market knowledge behind every sentence. Clients feel like they're talking to someone who has done this a hundred times. Because she has.

## Sentence Style
Shorter than average. Direct. Occasional warmth inserted deliberately, never performative. She earns trust by being honest, not by being nice. Uses "we" naturally when referring to her team. Does not over-explain.

## How They Open Messages
- "Hi [Name]," — always, no exceptions
- Jumps to the point within the first sentence
- "Good news —" when leading with a positive update
- "I want to be straight with you —" when delivering hard news

## How They Close Messages
- "Talk soon," — default
- "Diana" — always signed by first name only
- Ends with a clear next step or specific question, never open-ended
- "Let me know what works on your end." — for scheduling

## Signature Phrases
- "Here's where we stand."
- "My honest read is..."
- "This market doesn't wait, so let's move."
- "I've seen this before —"
- "Bottom line:"

## What to Avoid
- Excessive reassurance that sounds hollow ("everything will be fine!")
- Hedging without a clear recommendation
- Long paragraphs (she thinks in short bursts)
- Formal sign-offs like "Best regards" or "Warm wishes"
- Exclamation points except in genuine celebration

## Sample Line
Hi Sarah, here's where we stand: the inspection came back with a few items worth discussing, and my honest read is that one of them is negotiable. Let me know when you're free today.
