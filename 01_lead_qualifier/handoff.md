# Lead Qualifier Handoff

## What I Produce

I do not produce a standalone file. The Lead Card lives inside deal-file.md.

## What I Write

**1. Deal folder created:**
`_shared/cases/[LASTNAME-YEAR-###]/`

Contents copied from `_shared/cases/_template/`:
- deal-file.md (with Lead Card and first Session Log entry filled in)
- comms/ (empty folder, ready for 03_client_communication)

**2. Lead Card section of deal-file.md** — all seven fields filled:
- Intent
- Budget
- Timeline
- Location preferences
- Must-haves
- Deal-breakers
- Communication style

**3. First Session Log entry in deal-file.md:**
```
### [Date] — Session 1
**Agent:** [name]
**Stage at start:** new_lead
**Update from agent:** first session
**Work completed:** Lead Card written by 01_lead_qualifier
**Stage at end:** qualified
```

**4. Stage field in deal-file.md** set to: `qualified`
(or `NOT READY` if timeline >12 months or no clear budget — with note explaining why)

## What Comes Next

After the Lead Card is written, the orchestrator offers to route to 02_property_research. The agent decides whether to continue now or return later.
