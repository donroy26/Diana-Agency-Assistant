# Research Brief — Kevin Torres (TORRES-2026-003)
**Deal Type:** Buyer
**Date:** 2026-05-16
**Target areas:** East Austin (78721, 78722, 78702), Mueller
**Budget:** $425,000–$500,000

## Neighborhood Analysis

**Mueller** is the most walkable option in the budget: mixed-use development with parks, a farmers market, and retail within the community. New construction and well-maintained 2010s builds dominate. A 3/2 here runs $480–540k; anything under $500k in Mueller is a 2/2 or small 3/2 under 1,400 sq ft. Competitive segment. Homes move in 10–20 days.

**East Austin (78702/78721)** offers more variety and slightly more value per square foot. Older craftsman-style homes mixed with newer infill construction. Walkability is high near Manor Road and Airport Blvd corridors. In the $425–500k range, the client is looking at 3/2s in the 1,100–1,500 sq ft range, some with original character, some renovated. More buyer's side: days on market are running longer than Mueller (20–35 days in this range).

**78722 (Upper Cherrywood)** is the value play, slightly quieter, but walkable to Mueller and east of the university cluster. $425–500k gets more square footage here. Fewer bidding wars.

## Market Conditions

East Austin in the $425–500k range is slightly buyer-favoring right now. Average DOM is 22 days. Most homes are selling within 1–2% of list. Not a lot of bidding wars at this price point compared to 2022, but well-priced, renovated homes in 78702 still move fast. Mueller is tighter. Expect to compete.

## Budget Reality Check

$425–500k in East Austin or Mueller is realistic for a 3/2 under 1,500 sq ft. Kevin should expect to choose between size and proximity: walkable Mueller gets him less space. If he's firm on 3 bedrooms and walkability, $475k+ gives him more options. Under $450k narrows to 2/2 or properties needing work.
