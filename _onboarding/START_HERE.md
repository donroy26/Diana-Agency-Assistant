# Start Here

## What This Is

This is an AI assistant system for Diana's real estate team. You talk to it like a colleague. It helps you qualify new leads, research properties and neighborhoods, draft client messages in your voice, work through offers, and track transactions from contract to close. Everything runs inside Claude. No new software, no new logins.

## Before Your First Session

1. Install Claude Code at [claude.ai/code](https://claude.ai/code) — it's free
2. Run `install.bat` (Windows) — double-click it. It will set up your profile and create a shortcut on your desktop. If you're on a Mac, see the README for Mac setup.
3. Open the "Dianas Team AI" shortcut on your desktop

## Your First Session

Claude will ask your name, then walk you through a 5-minute voice setup. You'll paste a couple of messages you've sent to clients. Claude uses those to learn how you write, so everything it drafts sounds like you. After that you're operational and ready to start working deals.

## What You Can Ask It

- Qualifying a new lead (it walks you through the intake and builds the lead card)
- Researching a property or neighborhood in Austin
- Drafting a client email or text in your voice
- Working through an offer: structure, strategy, counter-offer scenarios
- Tracking a transaction: deadlines, documents, what's missing

## What It Cannot Do

- Send messages on your behalf (it drafts, you review and send)
- Access MLS, Zillow, or any live data directly (you describe what you've seen, it helps you analyze it)
- Give legal or contract advice (flag anything legal to Diana)
- Remember previous conversations on its own (the deal file is its memory). That's why every session starts with a brief update on what's happened since you last talked.

Ask Diana.
